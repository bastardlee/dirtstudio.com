URBAN OUTFITTERS HEADQUARTERS

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
2005 - 2011

For over one hundred and fifty years, League Island was transformed by the Navy, building huge ships and mooring entire fleets. Remaining true to the industrious scale of on-going ship building operations and to the patina of past production, the retail enterprise of Urban Outfitters will continue that transformation constructing their campus of creativity.

The renewed historic core of the Navy Yard enlivens industrial buildings retrofitted for design studios and corporate offices. The central dry dock serves as a public landing for the Broad Street civic axis terminating at the Delaware River. The landscape builds upon the industrial scale of the Yard while overlaying a rich texture of new growth, programmatic uses, and Urban Outfitterâ€™s sensibility.
