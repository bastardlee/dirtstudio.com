MASS MoCA

North Adams, Massachusetts
1997

Thirteen acres of giant factory buildings are bisected by the concrete channels of the Hoosiac River. The abandoned textile factory that once employed half the town now houses a contemporary art collection formerly hidden in storage. Vast industrial galleries are interwoven with commercial businesses and community services, revitalizing the town and integrating a network of art institutions within the region.

TEAM

Joe Thompson, director
and Sandy Frucher, chairman
Bruner/Cott, architects
